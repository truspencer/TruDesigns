All About Me


I currently study Animation and Motion Graphics and enjoy drawing and writing in my spare time. Learning about multimedia and how to create things that I see daily, has broaden my horizons and has helped me learn more about myself. I am eager to learn more and eventually work in the field. I consider myself to be a very unique. I am obsessed with anime and gaming and I try to show that throughout my everyday life. I enjoy creating projects that relfect my "obsessions" and showing how creative I can be when working with them. My interest on Graphic Design stems from my interest in becoming a Game Designer and how the two professions go hand in hand. Besides anime and gaming, I like to make projects that reflect my heritage. My family is half Native- American and I love to show that.


A lot of my artwork inspiration stems from animes I've watched and from my studies in Japanese culture. I enjoy drawing original characters either of my friends or of myself and even having one of my friends animate the characters for me. Currently a few friends and I are working on making our own anime series and video game with characters based on ourselves. I'm glad I am able to learn how to use some of the Adobe programs I have learned so far. I feel with my current level of knowledge of them, I will be able to help my friends with the production with more than just my artwork.
